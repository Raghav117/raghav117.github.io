Privacy Policy for Emergency Location

Last Updated: June 3, 2025

1. Purpose of the App

Emergency Location is designed to help users share their current location with pre-selected contacts during emergencies. The app does not require internet and uses SMS to send the location. This is done only when the user taps the notification, ensuring complete user control.

2. Permissions We Use and Why

a. ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION

This permission allows the app to access your deviceâ€™s location even when running in the background. It is only used when you tap the emergency notification to send your current location to trusted contacts.

b. SEND_SMS

This permission is used to send your location via SMS to contacts you have selected. No SMS is sent automatically or without your confirmation.

c. SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW

This permission allows us to show emergency controls (like floating buttons or overlays) on top of other apps. This ensures that you can quickly send your location even in critical situations.

3. Data Collection and Internet Usage

  • We do not collect or store any personal data, contacts, or messages.
  • We do not use internet access at all.
  • All data processing happens locally on your device.

4. User Control

You can manage permissions anytime via your device settings. Location and SMS are accessed only with your consent and only when you tap the emergency notification.

5. Security

Your data never leaves your device except through the SMS message you choose to send. We do not track, log, or store any data on servers.
